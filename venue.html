The ACM Summer School on Recommender Systems

Gothenburg, Sweden. September 9th - 13th, 2019.

Venue

The summer school will be organized at the University of Gothenburg in the centre of Gothenburg. The sessions on Monday and Tuesday, September 9-10, will be held at Wallenberg Conference Center. The sessions on Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday, September 11-13, will be held at Academicum. Both venues are within short walking distance of each other.
